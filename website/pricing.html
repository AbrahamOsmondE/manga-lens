FAQ

Common questions.

What counts as a "page translation"?
Each manga image translated counts as one page. The free tier allows 20 per day, resetting at midnight UTC. Most chapters are 20–30 pages, so the free tier covers about one chapter a day.
Which manga sites does it support?
Any site with manga images — MangaDex, fan scanlations, aggregators, and official publishers. If the page shows a manga image, MangaLens can translate it. There are no site-specific integrations.
How does the free trial work?
The Pro Monthly plan includes a 100%-off first month via coupon. Your card is charged nothing for the first 30 days. After that, $3.99/month unless you cancel.
Can I cancel any time?
Yes. Cancel from your Stripe customer portal (link in billing emails) and your Pro access continues until the end of the billing period. No questions asked.
How is the translation quality?
Translations use Google's Gemini model, which handles manga-specific language well — including sound effects, honorifics, and informal dialogue. Quality is significantly better than older machine translation approaches.
Is my data private?
Auth is handled entirely by Google OAuth — no passwords stored. Manga images are sent to the translation service for processing and are not stored. Your email is used only to track your subscription tier. Contact us with privacy questions.
