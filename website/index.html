How it works

Three steps. Then just read.

No setup wizard, no configuration. Install and you're reading in under a minute.

1

Install the extension

Add MangaLens to Chrome from the Web Store in one click. It's free — no account needed to install.

2

Sign in with Google

One tap auth. No passwords or extra accounts — your Google identity ties your free quota and subscription together.

3

Click "Enable Translation"

Navigate to any manga page and hit the button. Every speech bubble on the page translates automatically — including new ones as you scroll.

Features

Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

🌐

Works on any manga site

MangaDex, fan scanlations, official publishers — if the page has an image, MangaLens can translate it. No site-specific integrations required.

⚡

Real-time, as you scroll

New panels that load as you scroll are detected and translated automatically. Never wait for the whole page to queue up.

🤖

Powered by Gemini AI

Translation uses Google's Gemini model — accurate, context-aware, and handles manga-specific phrases, sound effects, and honorifics.

🔒

Privacy-first

Authentication is handled entirely by Google OAuth — no passwords stored, no third-party tracking. Your Google identity is verified on every request.
